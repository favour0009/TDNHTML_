WHY WAS THIS LIBRARY PUT UP

Good day everyone,my name is Nwachukwu Favour and i am the founder of this online library.
This Library was established for the sole purpose of learning.
Growing up i attended a public school and we were either lacking teachers or lacking textbooks.
Worst of it all we didn't have a library and this might have discouraged a lot people from reading back in my schooling days.
That was when i vowed to ensure that no one lacked the educational resources needed to learn.
And growing up I loved reading,it was either poetry or drama with me.
But the lack of text books really disturbed me due to the that i was in science class and my parents lacked the sufficient funds to buy them.

HOW THIS LIBRARY WORKS

The main function of this library is to ensure everyone learns.
So therefore, this website was put up to be an interactive learningenvironment.
We know no one is an island of knowledge so this website is one where you can come to share textbooks on various subjects and you can ask questions.
I assure you someone be online to answer you.

Thank you and let the learning commence.
